ABOUT ME

LORA

Lora, the talented and dedicated owner of "Lora Beauty," has created a haven for beauty and self-expression in her salon. With a passion for hair, she offers an array of services, from intricate braiding to luxurious hair treatments, ensuring her clients leave with a look that not only suits their individual style but also boosts their confidence. Lora's commitment to quality is evident not only in her services but also in the carefully selected beauty products, including luxury wigs, hair extensions, accessories, and African Ankara dresses, allowing clients to further enhance their beauty and style.
