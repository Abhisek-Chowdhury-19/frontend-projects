SERVICES

Hair braiding

Hair braiding is a timeless and versatile hairstyling technique that has been practiced for centuries across different cultures and regions. It involves weaving or interlacing strands of hair together to create intricate and visually appealing patterns. Braiding not only serves a functional purpose, such as keeping hair neat and organized, but it is also a form of artistic expression and a means of cultural identity.

SERVICES

Dreadlocks

Dreadlocks, also known as locs or dreads, are rope-like strands of hair formed by matting or braiding hair. They are one of the most versatile natural hairstyles. To keep dreadlocks healthy and hydrated, you should wash them regularly. You can also cleanse them with a detoxifying cleanser to remove any accumulated build-up. If you lead an active lifestyle, you should wash your locs once a week.

SERVICES

Afro Caribbean Hair Style

Afro Caribbean hair has a flat, twisted structure with a thin diameter. The twists change the width of the hair. The afro hairstyle is one of the top traditional Black hairstyles. It's often seen as cool, confident, and powerful. It has appeared at the Met Gala, the Oscars, and high fashion runway shows. Celebrities like Solange, Zendaya, and Viola Davis have embraced the look.

SERVICES

Curly perm

Curly perm, short for "permanent wave," is a popular hair treatment that has been cherished by many for decades. This transformative process involves chemically altering the structure of straight or naturally wavy hair to create lasting curls or waves. The result is a bouncy and voluminous mane that adds a touch of elegance and style to your overall appearance.

SERVICES

Weaving hairstyles

Weaving hairstyles, also known as weave hairstyles or hair weaves, are a popular and versatile way to change your hair's appearance and style. Weaves involve attaching artificial or natural hair extensions to your existing hair, allowing you to experiment with different lengths, textures, and colors.
