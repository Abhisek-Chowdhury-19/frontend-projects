ABOUT ME

LORA

SERVICES

Afro Caribbean Hair Style

Afro Caribbean hair has a flat, twisted structure with a thin diameter. The twists change the width of the hair. The afro hairstyle is one of the top traditional Black hairstyles. It's often seen as cool, confident, and powerful. It has appeared at the Met Gala, the Oscars, and high fashion runway shows. Celebrities like Solange, Zendaya, and Viola Davis have embraced the look.

SERVICES

Dreadlocks

Dreadlocks, also known as locs or dreads, are rope-like strands of hair formed by matting or braiding hair. They are one of the most versatile natural hairstyles. To keep dreadlocks healthy and hydrated, you should wash them regularly. You can also cleanse them with a detoxifying cleanser to remove any accumulated build-up. If you lead an active lifestyle, you should wash your locs once a week.

Lora's In-Home Services for Disabled Individuals

Lora's dedication to her clients goes beyond her salon's four walls. She also provides in-home services for disabled individuals, recognizing the importance of accessibility and convenience in the world of beauty and self-care. By extending her services to those with disabilities, Lora ensures that everyone has the opportunity to experience the transformational power of her expertise, bringing beauty and confidence directly to their doorstep. Her commitment to inclusivity and making her services accessible to all sets her apart as a compassionate and skilled beauty professional.
